Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the monthly lease rate?

Our monthly lease rates include the vehicle, comprehensive insurance, routine maintenance, and 24/7 roadside assistance. The only additional costs would be fuel and any damages not covered by insurance.

Can I change vehicles during my lease term?

Yes, we offer the flexibility to exchange your vehicle for a different model during your lease term, subject to availability and any rate differences.

What happens if the car needs repairs?

We cover all maintenance and repairs during your lease period. If your vehicle needs service, we'll provide a replacement vehicle while yours is being repaired.

Is there a mileage limit?

Our standard lease includes unlimited mileage within the Riviera Maya region. For travel outside this area, please discuss your plans with our team.
